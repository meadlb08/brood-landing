Company Overview

Brood Technologies, Inc. builds self-organizing swarms of unmanned underwater vehicles (UUVs) to transform how military and commercial operators monitor, secure, and explore the underwater environment. Our dual-use platforms are smaller, more scalable, and dramatically less expensive than traditional UUVs—enabling rapid, resilient underwater operations at scale.

Differentiators

  • First company purpose-built for UUV swarm operations
  • Self-organizing, non-hierarchical mesh networks for scalable sensing
  • Fully interoperable with Navy UxS C2 systems via UMAS architecture
  • Rapid prototyping with sub-30 day design-to-deployment cycle
  • Orders of magnitude lower cost than legacy ROV and UUV systems

Solutions & Use Cases

Autonomous Maritime Systems

  • Swarm-capable UUV design & manufacturing
  • Modular payload integration (optical, sonar, RF, environmental sensors)
  • AI-based autonomous mission planning and execution

Commercial Underwater Services

  • Environmental monitoring and seabed mapping
  • Coastal surveillance and marine asset protection
  • Port security and customs inspections
